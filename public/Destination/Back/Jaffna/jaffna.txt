Jaffna 
Jaffna, a cultural hub, enchants with its Tamil heritage and historic landmarks like Nallur Kandaswamy Temple. Families explore the vibrant markets, while adventure seekers visit nearby islands like Delft. Luxury travelers enjoy boutique hotels with colonial charm. Wellness retreats offer meditation in serene settings. The unique cuisine and warm hospitality make Jaffna a highlight for cultural heritage tours in Sri Lanka.
