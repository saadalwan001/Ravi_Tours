Tangalle’s pristine beaches and tranquil lagoons offer a serene escape for families and luxury travelers. Adventure seekers can surf at Medaketiya Beach or explore Rekawa’s turtle nesting sites. Cultural highlights include Mulkirigala Rock Temple, a historic gem. Wellness-focused resorts provide Ayurvedic treatments, promoting relaxation. Upscale villas with private chefs elevate the experience. Tangalle is a hidden gem for luxury travel in Sri Lanka’s southern coast.
