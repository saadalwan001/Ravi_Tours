Bentota

Bentota, a coastal gem, combines golden beaches with lush mangroves, ideal for family adventures. Water sports like jet-skiing thrill adventurers, while cultural tours to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery educate and inspire. Luxury travelers relax in five-star resorts with spa facilities. Wellness seekers enjoy yoga by the Bentota River. The vibrant markets and seafood dining add local flavor. Bentota is a top destination for Sri Lanka beach holidays.
