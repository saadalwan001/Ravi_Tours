Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a cradle of Sinhalese civilization with ancient stupas and sacred Bodhi trees. Families explore archaeological wonders, while cultural enthusiasts visit Mihintale, the birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka. Adventure seekers cycle through historic trails. Luxury travelers enjoy guided tours and upscale resorts. Wellness retreats offer meditation in serene settings. Anuradhapura is a cornerstone of cultural heritage sites in Sri Lanka.
