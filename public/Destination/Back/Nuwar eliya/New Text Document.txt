Nuwara Eliya 
Known as “Little England,” Nuwara Eliya captivates with its cool climate, colonial architecture, and sprawling tea estates. Families can tour tea factories or stroll through Victoria Park, while adventure seekers hike to Horton Plains’ World’s End. Luxury travelers indulge in high tea at historic hotels like the Grand. Wellness retreats in serene surroundings promote relaxation. The vibrant Gregory Lake offers boating, perfect for group activities. Nuwara Eliya is a highlight for luxury travel in Sri Lanka’s hill country.
