Dambulla 
Dambulla’s Golden Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, boasts ancient cave temples adorned with intricate murals and Buddha statues. This spiritual hub offers families a deep dive into Sri Lanka’s Buddhist heritage. Adventure travelers can explore nearby Pidurangala Rock for stunning views. Luxury experiences include private cultural tours and stays in eco-friendly resorts. The surrounding countryside provides serene settings for wellness retreats. Dambulla is a cornerstone of cultural heritage sites in Sri Lanka.
